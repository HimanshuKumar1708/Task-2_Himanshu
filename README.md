# Data Cleaning & Analysing SQL

# Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing an E-commerce sales dataset using SQL queries in Python to extract meaningful business insights.

# Objectives
 . Analyze product sales performance
 . Calculate product-wise revenue
 . Identify customer spending patterns
 . Analyze payment method preferences
 . Track monthly revenue trends

# Tools & Technologies
 . Python . Pandas . (SQL)SQLite . Jupyter Notebook

# Dataset Features
 . CustomerID
 . Product
 . Quantity
 . UnitPrice
 . TotalPrice
 . PaymentMethod
 . CouponCode
 . OrderStatus
 . ReferralSource
 . Date

# SQL Analysis Performed
 . Loaded cleaned dataset into SQLite database
 . Executed SQL queries using Python
 . Identified top-selling products
 . Calculated highest revenue-generating products
 . Analyzed payment method usage
 . Generated monthly revenue trends
 . Identified top spending customers

# Key SQL Operations
 . SELECT
 . GROUP BY
 . ORDER BY
 . SUM()
 . COUNT()
 . LIMIT
 . strftime() (Date Analysis)

# Results
 . Identified best-selling products
 . Calculated product-wise revenue
 . Analyzed customer spending behavior
 . Determined preferred payment methods
 . Generated monthly sales insights for business decisions
